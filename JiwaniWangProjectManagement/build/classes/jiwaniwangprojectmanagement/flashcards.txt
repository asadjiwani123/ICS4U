What is the name of the method used in the SDLC?
Answer: Waterfall Model
What are the phases of the waterfall model?
Answer: Problem statement, Analysis, Design, Implementation, Integration/Test, Maintenance
Which phase of the waterfall model has the highest cost when it comes to developing software
Answer: Maintenance phase
Who created the Waterfall Model and when did he/she create it?
Answer: Dr. Winston Royce created it in 1970
Who created the Grantt Chart?
Answer: Henry Grantt
What is the purpose of the Grantt Chart?
Answer: It helps programmers visualize and track the schedule and actual progress of projects
What does UML stand for?
Answer: Unified Modeling Language
